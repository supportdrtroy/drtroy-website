About Dr. Troy Hounshell

Bringing decades of clinical expertise to continuing education

Dr. Troy Hounshell brings over 25 years of clinical experience to continuing education. With a Doctor of Science in Physical Therapy from Texas Tech University Health Sciences Center, he has worked across diverse healthcare settings including outpatient clinics, hospitals, home health, and skilled nursing facilities.

As CEO of Texas Therapy Pros, operating 5 locations across West Texas, Dr. Hounshell understands the real-world challenges healthcare professionals face. His courses bridge the gap between academic theory and practical application.

Every course is designed with the busy healthcare professional in mind, providing evidence-based content that directly applies to daily practice across all care settings.
