## üìä Core Business Metrics

### Revenue & Growth
- **Monthly Revenue** - Track total income from course sales
- **Average Order Value (AOV)** - Revenue per transaction
- **Revenue per Customer** - Lifetime value tracking
- **Growth Rate** - Month-over-month and year-over-year
- **Conversion Rate** - Visitors ‚Üí paying customers
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)** - Marketing spend per new customer

### Customer Metrics
- **New Customers** - First-time purchasers per month
- **Repeat Customers** - Return purchase rate
- **Customer Retention** - How many come back
- **Refund Rate** - Percentage of refunds/complaints
- **Net Promoter Score (NPS)** - Customer satisfaction surveys

## üéØ Marketing Effectiveness

### Discount Code Performance
- **Code Conversion Rate** - Which codes drive most sales
- **Most Popular Codes** - Which promotions work best  
- **Code ROI** - Revenue generated vs discount given
- **Organic vs Discounted Sales** - Percentage paying full price
- **Employee vs Marketing Code Usage** - Internal vs external usage

### Traffic & Acquisition
- **Website Visitors** - Unique visitors per month
- **Traffic Sources** - Direct, referral, social, email
- **Landing Page Performance** - Which pages convert best
- **Geographic Distribution** - Where customers are located
- **Device Usage** - Mobile vs desktop preferences

## üìö Course & Content Metrics

### Engagement
- **Course Completion Rate** - % who finish courses
- **Average Time to Complete** - How long courses take
- **Most Popular Courses** - Which content performs best
- **Drop-off Points** - Where people quit courses
- **Certificate Download Rate** - Final completion metric

### Usage Patterns  
- **Peak Usage Times** - When people take courses
- **Session Duration** - How long users stay active
- **Return Visits** - How often they come back
- **Mobile Usage** - Percentage on phones/tablets
